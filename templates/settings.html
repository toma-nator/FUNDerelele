Contribution Room

Room is calculated automatically from your contribution transactions and shown on each TFSA / FHSA / RDSP account page. RDSP needs no input — it tracks the $200,000 lifetime cap from your contributions.

Birth year sets when your TFSA room started accruing (year you turned 18, earliest 2009). FHSA infers its open year from your first contribution. Limits for 2009–{{ tfsa_builtin_through }} are built in — you only need an override for a brand-new year (or to correct a past year). Comma-separate multiple, e.g. 2027=7500.

Enter the room CRA reports in My Account as of Jan 1 of that year. The app rolls it forward — adding later years' limits and subtracting contributions since — so update it each January.
